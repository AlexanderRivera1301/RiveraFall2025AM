Welcome to Mushroom World

Mushrooms are fascinating organisms that have been used for food, medicine, and spiritual practices for centuries. Some even get cool nicknames for their "powers"!

I made this site to show of some of the most interesting mushrooms to me, their classifications, and their uses. Some are edible, while others are toxic.

Did You Know?

The largest living organism on Earth is a mushroom! A honey fungus in Oregon spans over 2,385 acres.
